About DataCompound

DataCompound is a boutique data & AI practice led by Diogo Souza, a data engineer with nearly 15 years of experience across aviation, finance, government, and infrastructure. We specialize in bridging Business Intelligence and Machine Learning so analytics and AI grow on the same solid foundation.
